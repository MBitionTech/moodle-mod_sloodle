Stipend Giver

This is a sub-type of the SLOODLE module, allowing you to setup a means to pay a certain amount of Linden Dollars to your students in OpenSimulator. In order to make the transfer, you will need to setup a SLOODLE Stipend Giver object in OpenSimulator as well, and connect it to your Stipend Giver in Moodle. Students can then click the object in OpenSimulator to receive their payment.
